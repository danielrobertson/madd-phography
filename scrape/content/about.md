## Welcome to Madd Photography
Welcome to my page! My name is Madeleine + I am a Psychology major with a minor in business at Abilene Christian University. I was born and raised in El Paso, TX, but have resided in the DFW area.

I found myself with a passion for photography when I attended a workshop held by Asia Joy, Owner of Photo By Joy. All it took was one workshop and I was hooked. I was constantly craving more information and wanted to learn everything I could. After four years of attending photography workshops I finally invested in myself and purchased my own camera and then begun Madd Photography.

## Hobbies
Photography is something I enjoy with every ounce of my being, but it is not all to me. I love working out and talking about love languages. I love discovering new music and having dance parties. Some of my favorite artists are Faye Webster, Weezer, SZA, Bad Bunny + Harry Styles.

## Photography Style
I am all about being in the moment. I try to guide you, but not pose you. Most people have the fear of looking awkward in pictures because of prior photography experiences. I am here to have fun and make you look GOOD.
